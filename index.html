Welcome to HandsFree.io

A Program which can help people with disability to operate computer systems, just by using their facial expressions. A highly interactive and easy-to-use program. Download now to get started!

About Us

In recent years, a number of researchers are developing different kinds of assistive devices for differently-abled people. In this project, we will be using the movement of illuminant markers through facial expressions to control the cursor movement in computer applications. In our project we will be using a set of five facial expressions for controlling the cursor movement in the left and right direction, up and down and click, respectively. How it Works?

1

Open mouth to activate cursor

2

Blink right eye for right click

3

Blink left eye for left click

4

Squint eyes to activate scroll

5

Move your head up and down to move cursor vertically

6

Move your head right to left to move cursor horizontally
